Our Story &
Our Mission

For over four decades, MMBA has been the heartbeat of Maharashtrian culture in Northern California — a home away from home for thousands of families.

Who We Are

Maharashtra Mandal
Bay Area

Maharashtra Mandal Bay Area (MMBA) is a registered non-profit organization that has served the Maharashtrian community in Northern California since 1980. We are a community of immigrants, dreamers, engineers, artists, and families who share a deep love for Maharashtrian culture.

Our mission is to promote cultural ties among people interested in Maharashtrian culture from various parts of the world — and to pass that rich heritage to the next generation growing up in Silicon Valley.

We believe culture is not just about the past — it's a living, breathing source of identity, pride, and community.

Our Core Mission
"To promote cultural ties among people interested in Maharashtrian culture and to encourage the next generation to learn, celebrate, and carry forward their heritage."

What We Do

Our Four Pillars

Every MMBA activity falls under one of four pillars that together make up the full tapestry of Maharashtrian cultural life.

🎭
Cultural Events

We organize the full spectrum of Maharashtrian festivals: Makar Sankrant, Ganeshotsav, Wari, Mangalagaur, Kojagiri, and Diwali. These festivals give our community a chance to renew cultural ties and encourage children to learn about Maharashtrian culture in a fun, immersive way.

🎶
Arts & Music

We provide a platform for local talents in music, dance, and acting through performances, competitions, and presentations in the performing arts. We also arrange programs of visiting artists from India, giving Bay Area Maharashtrians access to world-class cultural entertainment.

🎪
Theater

Maharashtra's modern theater tradition dates back over 100 years. Keeping this tradition alive, MMBA presents an annual Marathi play performed by talented local artists. Occasionally, visiting theatrical groups from India also perform at our venue, bringing fresh stories and energy.

🤝
Social Impact

We work hand in hand with organizations dedicated to running community service programs in India. Revenue generated from select programs is donated for this cause — connecting our prosperous Bay Area community with the need back home and giving back meaningfully.

Our History

Four Decades of
Cultural Pride

From a small gathering of Maharashtrian families to a thriving 1,000+ member organization, MMBA has grown with Silicon Valley.

1980
MMBA Founded

A group of Maharashtrian families in the Bay Area come together to form Maharashtra Mandal Bay Area, establishing a cultural home for the growing community.

1985
First Annual Ganeshotsav

MMBA organizes its first large-scale Ganeshotsav celebration, which becomes the organization's flagship event drawing hundreds of families.

1992
501(c)(3) Non-Profit Status

MMBA receives official non-profit status, formalizing its mission and enabling broader community programs and charitable giving.

2000
Theater Program Launched

The annual Marathi Natak program is established, offering a stage for local theatrical talent and bringing professional Marathi theater to Silicon Valley.

2010
Digital Expansion

MMBA launches its online presence, making it easier for Maharashtrians across the Bay Area to connect, register, and stay updated on events.

2024
BMM2024 — Silicon Valley

The global Bharatiya Marathi Mandal convention comes to San Jose, bringing the worldwide Maharashtrian community together in Silicon Valley.
