Our Community

Maharashtrian families, professionals, artists, and dreamers — all finding a second home in Silicon Valley through the warmth of shared culture.

Who We Are

Maharashtrians
of Silicon Valley

The Bay Area is home to one of the largest concentrations of Maharashtrian professionals, families, and students outside of India. MMBA has been their cultural anchor since 1980.

Our community spans generations — from first-generation immigrants who brought Maharashtrian culture with them, to second-generation children discovering their roots, to families who've never left Maharashtra but love what we do.

What unites us is a shared love of the Marathi language, Maharashtrian food, music, dance, theater, and the warmth of community.

What We Offer

Programs for Every
Member of the Family

Whether you're 6 or 60, MMBA has something for you — cultural, social, artistic, and charitable programs across the year.

👧
For Children

Cultural programs, festival activities, and children's performances designed to help the second generation connect with their Maharashtrian roots in a fun, engaging way.

👩
For Women

Mangalagaur celebrations, Kojagiri evenings, and women-led cultural events that honor feminine traditions and create spaces for community bonding.

🧑‍💼
For Professionals

Networking opportunities within the Maharashtrian professional community in Silicon Valley — because shared culture is the best foundation for business connections.

🎨
For Artists

A stage, an audience, and a community. MMBA actively seeks out and celebrates local talent in music, dance, theater, and the visual arts.

🌍
For Givers

MMBA channels community resources into charitable programs in India. Volunteers and donors help fund social service organizations doing meaningful work.

👴
For Seniors

MMBA events are intergenerational — seniors find connection, familiarity, and the joy of sharing their cultural wisdom with younger generations.
