🪔 Ganeshotsav

MMBA's grandest 10-day festival — celebrating Lord Ganesh with daily aarti, cultural programs, children's competitions, dhol-tasha, and the beloved visarjan procession.

About This Festival

Ganapati Bappa Morya!

Ganeshotsav is MMBA's flagship annual event — a joyful 10-day celebration of Lord Ganesh featuring Ganesh Sthapana (installation), daily morning and evening aarti, cultural performances by local artists and children, Marathi plays, bhajans, dhol-tasha processions, traditional prasad (modak, puran poli), and Visarjan on the final day. MMBA organizes Ganesh competitions for kids including aarti recitation, rangoli, and drawing. The festival is open to all Bay Area residents.

2024
📅 Sep 7 – Sep 16, 2024
📍 Mt. Pleasant High School, 1750 S White Rd, San Jose, CA 95127
Past Edition

MMBA Ganeshotsav 2024 was a spectacular 10-day celebration at Mt. Pleasant High School. The air was filled with Bappa's aura from day one, with an electrifying Ganesh Sthapana followed by nightly cultural programs. 2024 featured a special Dhol-Tasha Pathak procession, children's competitions, and a visiting artist performance that drew hundreds from across the Bay Area.

  • Ganesh Sthapana with traditional puja rituals
  • 10 nights of cultural programs — music, dance, drama
  • Dhol-Tasha Pathak procession on select evenings
  • Children's competitions: aarti, rangoli, drawing
  • Maharashtrian food stalls — modak, misal, vada pav
  • Visarjan procession on Anant Chaturdashi (Sep 16)

2021
📅 Sep 10 – Sep 19, 2021
📍 Virtual / Hybrid — Zoom & Facebook Live
Virtual Edition

Ganeshotsav 2021 was organized as a hybrid virtual event due to pandemic restrictions. MMBA invited Bay Area families to recite aarti from their homes on video, which was shared on MMBA's Facebook Live and Zoom. Members were also given the opportunity to perform live on Zoom. A remarkable show of community resilience.

  • Home aarti video submissions by families across the Bay Area
  • Live performances streamed on Facebook and Zoom
  • Children's virtual rangoli & drawing competition
  • Online cultural program on Zoom — music, poetry, drama
  • MMBA coordinated and produced the entire event digitally

2020
📅 Aug 22 – Sep 1, 2020
📍 Virtual — Facebook Live & Zoom
Virtual Edition

Ganeshotsav 2020 was MMBA's first fully virtual edition, created in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. Performances were telecasted live on MMBA's Facebook page and Zoom — not pre-recorded but truly live, maintaining the spirit of the celebration. A golden opportunity for Bay Area members to showcase their talent from home.
