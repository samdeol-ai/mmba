Our Community

Rooted in Tradition,
Thriving in the Bay

Maharashtra Mandal Bay Area (MMBA) is a non-profit organization serving Northern California since 1980. Our mission is to promote cultural ties among people interested in Maharashtrian culture from various parts of the world.

  • 🌿Preserve Heritage — Keep Marathi language, customs, and traditions alive for the next generation.
  • 🌉Bridge Generations — Connect Bay Area-born children with their Maharashtrian roots.
  • 🌍Give Back — Partner with social organizations running programs in India.
  • 🎤Platform for Talent — Showcase local artists in music, dance, acting, and theater.
